## 1. Account Types and Eligibility

### 1.1 Current Account (Everyday Banking)

Contoso Financial Services offers a range of current accounts to meet the needs of our personal and business customers. To open a current account, applicants must:

- Be aged 18 years or older (16+ for basic accounts with parental consent)
- Provide proof of identity (passport, driving licence, or national identity card)
- Provide proof of address dated within the last three months (utility bill, bank statement, or council tax letter)
- Be a UK resident or have a valid UK address

**Standard Current Account Features:**
- No monthly fee for maintaining a zero or positive balance
- Contactless Visa debit card included (contactless limit: £100 per transaction)
- Access to online banking and mobile app (iOS and Android)
- Faster Payments, CHAPS, and BACS support
- Standing orders and direct debits
- Overdraft facility available subject to credit assessment

**Premium Current Account (Contoso Plus):**
- Monthly fee: £15 per month (waived if monthly deposits exceed £2,500)
- Travel insurance included for account holder and family
- Mobile phone insurance up to £1,500 replacement value
- Commission-free foreign currency transactions up to £500/month
- Dedicated relationship manager for queries

### 1.2 Savings Accounts

Contoso offers multiple savings products to help customers grow their money:

**Instant Access Savings Account:**
- Interest Rate: 3.5% AER (variable)
- Minimum opening balance: £1
- Maximum balance: £500,000

**Fixed Rate Bond (1 Year):**

**Fixed Rate Bond (2 Year):**
- Interest Rate: 4.5% AER (fixed for 24 months)

- Interest paid: Annually or at maturity

**Cash ISA (Individual Savings Account):**
- Interest Rate: 3.8% AER (variable, tax-free)
- Annual ISA allowance: £20,000 per tax year (2024/25)
- No tax on interest earned
- Transfers in accepted from other ISA providers
- Flexible ISA: withdraw and re-deposit within same tax year

**Junior ISA:**
- Interest Rate: 4.0% AER (variable, tax-free)
- Maximum annual contribution: £9,000 (2024/25 tax year)
- Available for children under 18

## 2. Overdraft Policies

### 2.1 Arranged Overdraft

An arranged overdraft allows you to borrow money through your current account up to an agreed limit. This must be applied for in advance.

**Eligibility:**
- Must hold an active Contoso current account for at least 3 months
- Subject to creditworthiness assessment
- Must be 18 years or older with UK residency

**Interest and Charges:**
- Interest Rate: 39.9% EAR (variable)
- No daily usage fee for arranged overdrafts
- Interest calculated daily and charged monthly
- No arrangement fee for standard limits up to £2,000
- Arrangement fee of £25 for limits between £2,000 and £5,000

**Limits:**
- Minimum arranged overdraft: £100
- Maximum arranged overdraft: £5,000 (subject to credit assessment)
- Higher limits available for Contoso Plus account holders

**How to Apply:**
- Online via the Contoso app (Settings > Overdraft > Apply Now)
- By calling 0800-CONTOSO (0800-266-8676) Monday to Friday 8am–8pm, Saturday 9am–5pm
- In branch at any Contoso Financial Services location

### 2.2 Unarranged Overdraft

Using your account beyond your arranged overdraft limit or when no overdraft is in place is considered an unarranged overdraft.

- Unarranged Overdraft Rate: 39.9% EAR
- Monthly Maximum Charge: £20 (in line with FCA regulations)
- We will send an alert notification before charging unarranged overdraft fees
- Payments may be returned unpaid if they would cause you to exceed our unarranged limit

## 4. Dispute Resolution and Complaints

### 4.1 Transaction Disputes

If you believe a transaction on your account is incorrect or unauthorised, please follow these steps:

**Step 1: Report Immediately**
Contact us as soon as you notice the disputed transaction. The sooner you report, the better we can protect your account.
- Phone: 0800-CONTOSO (24/7 for card disputes)
- App: Go to Transactions > Select Transaction > Report a Problem
- Online banking: My Account > Help > Dispute a Transaction

**Step 2: Investigation**
Once reported, our disputes team will:
- Acknowledge your report within 1 business day
- Issue a provisional credit within 5 business days for amounts over £50
- Complete investigation within 15 business days (up to 45 days for complex cross-border disputes)
- Provide written outcome with full explanation

**Step 3: Resolution**
- If the dispute is upheld: the amount is permanently credited to your account
- If the dispute is not upheld: we will explain our reasoning and inform you of your right to appeal

**Chargeback Rights:**
Purchases made on your Contoso Visa debit card may be eligible for chargeback under Visa rules if:
- The goods or services were not received
- The transaction was duplicated
- The merchant went into administration

### 4.2 Formal Complaints Process

Contoso Financial Services takes complaints seriously. We are committed to resolving all complaints fairly and promptly.

**How to Make a Complaint:**
- Email: complaints@contoso-financial.co.uk
- Phone: 0800-CONTOSO, ask for the Complaints Team
- In writing: Contoso Financial Services, Complaints Department, 1 Contoso Square, London, EC2A 1AA
- Online form: www.contoso-financial.co.uk/complaints

**Our Commitments:**
- Acknowledge your complaint within 5 business days
- Provide a final response within 8 weeks
- If we cannot resolve your complaint within 3 business days, we will send a written acknowledgement explaining the process

**Financial Ombudsman Service (FOS):**
If you are not satisfied with our response, or if 8 weeks have passed without resolution, you have the right to refer your complaint to the Financial Ombudsman Service free of charge.
- Website: www.financial-ombudsman.org.uk
- Phone: 0800 023 4567
- The FOS is available to eligible complainants who are private individuals or small businesses

### 5.2 Reporting a Lost or Stolen Card

If your card is lost or stolen, act immediately:

1. Freeze your card via the Contoso app (instant effect)
2. Call 0800-CONTOSO (available 24/7)
3. We will cancel your card and issue a replacement within 3–5 business days
4. Emergency cash can be arranged if you are abroad

You will not be liable for any unauthorised transactions made after you report the card lost or stolen, provided you have acted without undue delay.

### 5.3 Card Activation

New cards must be activated before use:
- Via the Contoso app: Cards > Activate New Card
- By making your first chip and PIN purchase at a retailer
- By calling 0800-CONTOSO and following the automated instructions

## 7. Online and Mobile Banking Security

### 7.1 Security Features

Contoso Financial Services employs multiple layers of security to protect your account:

- Two-factor authentication (2FA) required for all online logins
- Biometric authentication supported (Face ID, fingerprint)
- Real-time fraud detection using machine learning
- Automatic session timeout after 10 minutes of inactivity
- End-to-end encryption for all data transmissions

### 7.2 Password Reset and Account Recovery

If you have forgotten your password or are locked out of your account:

**Via the Contoso App:**
1. Open the app and tap "Forgot Password?"
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Receive a one-time code to your registered mobile number
4. Follow the instructions in the email link to create a new password
5. Your new password must be at least 12 characters, containing uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols

**Via Phone:**
- Call 0800-CONTOSO and select option 1 for account access issues
- Our team will verify your identity using our standard security questions and knowledge-based authentication
- A temporary password will be issued to your registered email

**Account Lockout:**
- After 5 failed login attempts, your account is temporarily locked for 30 minutes
- After 10 consecutive failed attempts, the account is permanently locked and requires identity verification to unlock

### 7.3 Reporting Fraud and Suspicious Activity

If you suspect your account has been compromised or you have been a victim of fraud:

1. Call our 24/7 fraud line: 0800-FRAUD-HELP (0800-372-834-357) immediately
2. Freeze your card via the app if you have access
3. Do not transfer funds to any "safe account" — this is always a scam
4. We will never ask for your full PIN or password over the phone

## 8. Regulatory Information and Customer Rights

### 8.1 Financial Services Compensation Scheme (FSCS)

Your eligible deposits with Contoso Financial Services are protected up to a total of £85,000 by the Financial Services Compensation Scheme (FSCS). The FSCS is an independent body set up to protect customers of authorised financial services firms.

- Coverage: £85,000 per eligible depositor, per authorised firm
- Joint accounts: £170,000 combined
- Temporary high balance protection: Up to £1 million for up to 6 months (for life events such as property sale, divorce settlement, or inheritance)

Contoso Financial Services is authorised and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) and the Prudential Regulation Authority (PRA).
